ℹ️ About SeaBorne Navigation Suite

A professional toolkit for marine towing and salvage operations with enhanced mission planning capabilities.

Features:

  • Coordinate format conversion (Decimal ↔ DDM)
  • Advanced mission planning with fleet selection
  • Real-time cost estimation and breakdown
  • Weather impact calculations
  • Multi-vessel specifications and performance modeling
  • Priority-based mission classification
  • Comprehensive fuel consumption and labor cost calculations
  • Google Maps integration
  • Mobile-friendly interface
